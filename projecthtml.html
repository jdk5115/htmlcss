Double Triple Choclate Cake Recipe

This is a recipe for a cake. I really like cake because it tastes good and is full of sugar. When you're done it should look like this:

You will need the following ingredients to make the cake.

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Subscribe to my email list to receive more great recipes!

Once you have the ingredients, follow the directions below:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees f.🔥🔥🔥
  2. Take the flour and sugar and combine the in a bowl. Stir well. 🥣
  3. Slowly stir in the milk while you continue to stir.
  4. Mix the eggs and vanilla together in a seperate bowl and beat the eggs. 🥚🥚
  5. Slowly stir in egg mixture
  6. Once all ingredients are added whip them with a mixer until batter is similar to pancake mix.
  7. Pour batter into a greased 9" by 9" baking pan
  8. Put in oven for 24 minutes ⏲
  9. Take out and let it cool for 5 minutes 🆒
  10. Enjoy!
